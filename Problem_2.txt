AI Resume Analysis Chat - Coding Assessment 2 [30-40 mins]
 
Develop a Streamlit app that allows users to:
Upload multiple resumes (PDF/Word).
Chat with an AI to analyze and compare resumes.
Use RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) to fetch relevant resume details in responses.
 
Evaluation Criteria :
Upload and process multiple resumes (PDF/Word).
Extract and store resume text for querying.
RAG-based chatbot to answer questions about resumes.
AI-generated candidate summaries and insights.
Bonus: Implement ranking to identify the best-fit candidate for a given role.
